Somewhere out in the Amber Wasteland, a vicious presence begins to rise. Reports of monsters coming from the abandoned mines grows with each passing day.
A small Outpost, right outside of the mouth of the cave, is the only line of defense against these foes. One day, a Hero arrives to the Outpost, in search of answers in the depths below...

HOW TO PLAY

Controls (Gameplay)

Input Action
W/A/S/D Move
Left Click Attack
Right Click Mine
1/2/3/4 Cast Skill
Space Dodge
Q Consume Stemkit (Heal)
E Interact/Collect

Controls (Menus)

Input Action
Left Click Select
Right Click Fast Equip (In Inventory)
I Open Inventory
C Open Stats
P Pause Game

DELVE is an Action Role-Playing Game (ARPG) about reaching the bottom of a monster-infested cave. Your goal is to find the exit leading to the next floor by mining through the cave walls.
